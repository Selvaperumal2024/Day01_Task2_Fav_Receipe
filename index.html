Vannila Icecream !!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup cold milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 1 pint heavy cream

Preparation Steps:

  1. Step 1: Stir together condensed milk, cold milk, vanilla, and salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  2. Step 2: Beat heavy cream in a large bowl with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form. Fold milk mixture into whipped cream.
  3. Step 3: Pour into a shallow 2-quart dish, cover, and freeze for 4 hours, stirring once after 2 hours or when edges start to harden.
  4. Step 4: Serve or store in an airtight container for up to 10 days.
